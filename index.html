About Us:

We are a small family business that provides indoor sleepover tent hire for children in the Neath Port Talbot area. We have been running since 2021. Our family consists of myself, Daniel, my wife harriet, our 2 young boys and our baby girl. We aim to provide a better sleepover experience by supplying larger than average tents with full size single airbeds. Our customers always give positive feedback and say they had a good nights sleep!
